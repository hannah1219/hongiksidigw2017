  • Studio 23:00
  • Naeun Kim, Jihoon Kim

    • 23시 스튜디오는 김나은과 김지훈이 함께 꾸려 나가는 그래픽 기반의 스튜디오이다. 우리는 밤을 사랑하고 삶의 위트를 잃지 않으려 하며, 사람들과 함께 하는 시간을 좋아하고 놀이하는 것을 즐긴다. 우리는 놀이와 참여를 통해 사람들과 관계 맺는 디자인을 지향한다.

    Studio 23:00 is a graphic design studio. We love our nights; we strive not to lose our passion for wit; we enjoy our time with others. We aim for design that enables us to engage with people through playing and participation.

    Even for dreamers, morning comes everyday. But we still need some time to Miss last night. The way you enjoy your morning is a little different With ASAKO BASAKO. ASAKO BASAKO suggest unique breakfast for dreamers based on experimental spirit and humor.

    WITTY BITTY BITES is a non-food allergens dessert brand. Just enjoy various flavors of cookies freely as we have solved the problem regarding nutritional imbalance as well. We hope that every children could grow up with confidence and courage. The food allergy won't be able to hinder children's dream anymore with WITTY BITTY BITES.

We catch customer's personality and lifestyle through a test and offer the scent of the star depending on the test result. We will make you shine like the stars in the night sky.
